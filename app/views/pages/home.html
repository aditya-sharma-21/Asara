Our Vision

Our vision is bringing together a sense of mutual respect for people. That involves ensuring their fundamental rights for them. We know this is going to be a long journey as we’ve started with limited resources but unlimited will power to bring change. To achieve this sense of mutual respect, we have to work on these three ethics.

  • Empowerment

    • Empowerment is a multi-dimensional social process that helps people gain control over their lives. It is multi-dimensional because works on different dimensions of an individual such as sociological, psychological, economic and other such dimensions. Social because it gives us a sense of involvement in society, being a social animal. It’s a process that fosters power in people, so that they can use it in their own life, community and society.

  • Justice

    • Justice, a basic human right. We don’t expect people to fight for it. To live a life that’s fulfilling, it’s necessary to have a sense of belief in our judiciary system and that supports respect in society. Human behavior is a realization of some natural laws, Justice is one of them. We believe justice can exist only within the parameters of equality. To achieve our vision and to bring a sense of security, we have to systematize social and economic inequalities.

  • Welfare

    • Public aid for children and elderly in need to support their basic necessities like health, education, habitat, rehabilitation, skill development and financial support. We will make efforts to fulfill these basic but important needs for them. Mental wellbeing of a person affects one’s behavior towards society. Our aim is to help them live a better livelihood.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring awareness among people, a sense of social consciousness. Consciousness will make people capable of making the right decisions for their own wellbeing and to spread awareness about financial aid, elementary education, rehabilitation, health services available to them. People are unaware of their own rights, services available to them. Our efforts will be to educate them.

Our Approach

Thanks to our volunteers, we’ve started, with limited resources but unlimited passion. With support of authorities, govt. bodies, local groups and organizations we are working to eliminate impoverishment and arrange basic necessities for children. The idea is to enact the plans on ground and keep encouraging people to do so. Our people, our volunteers are working hard to achieve the vision we have. Small but effective steps to make an Impact!
